Social Media Do's:

**Enable Privacy Settings: Regularly check who can see your personal posts.
*Fact-Check Before Sharing: Verify news stories to avoid spreading misinformation.
*Maintain Professionalism: Use a professional profile picture on platforms like LinkedIn.
*Network Wisely: Connect with VIT Bhopal alumni and industry professionals.
*Secure Your Accounts: Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA.

Social Media Don'ts:

*Don't Share Personal Info: Never post your home address, phone number, or OTPs.
*Don't Engage in Cyberbullying: Avoid heated arguments or offensive comments.
*Don't Click Suspicious Links: Beware of "too good to be true" offers in DMs.
*Don't Post Explicit Content: Avoid anything that could negatively impact your future career.
*Don't Overshare Location: Avoid tagging your exact real-time location on public posts.
